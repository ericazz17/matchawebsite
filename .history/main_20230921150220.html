A Matcha Lesson

Matcha is a vibrant green tea known for its finely ground powder form and unique preparation method. It's prized for its rich cultural history, impressive health benefits, and the calming ritual it offers.

History of Matcha

Matcha originated in China and was later embraced by Japanese Zen monks for its meditative and concentration-enhancing qualities. It became integral to Japanese tea ceremonies, evolving into a symbol of artistry and refinement. Today, matcha is celebrated globally for its vibrant green hue, unique flavor, and health benefits.
