Sifter Using a sifter is important when preparing matcha to ensure a smooth and clump-free consistency by breaking up any powder lumps, resulting in a more enjoyable and visually appealing matcha drink. To use a sifter, simply place the matcha powder in the sifter, then tap or gently shake it over your matcha bowl to evenly distribute the powder and eliminate any clumps. Row 1, Column 4
